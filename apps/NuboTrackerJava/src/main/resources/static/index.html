NuboMedia Tracker Example

This application shows a WebRtcEndpoint connected to itself (loopback) with a Tracker filter in the middle To run this demo follow these steps:


  1. Open this page with a browser (webRTC compliant)
  2. Press the Start button
  3. Grant the access to the camera and microphone.
  4. Choose the right configuration and apply it.
  5. Click on Stop to finish the communication.

About the configuration:
  • Threshold:this tracker is based on difference of images, thus to make the difference between corresponding pixels of two images, there is movement in the image when the absolute value of the difference exceeds a certain threshold.
  • Min Area Objects with a smaller area than the configured will be ruled out
  • Max Area Objects with a larger area than the configured will be ruled out
  • Distance Objects whose centers are located at a distance less than the setting, will join to form the same object

Threshold 20
Min Area 50
